- Extract the .zip somewhere on your computer.
- Run the executable to run the game.

================================================================================
2. UPDATING THE GAME
You have the following options to download potential new releases:
- Get it from https://nalquas.itch.io/blobs-of-colour
- Get it from https://github.com/nalquas/blobs-of-colour/releases
- Build it yourself using the source, available at https://github.com/nalquas/blobs-of-colour

================================================================================
3. HOW TO PLAY
- Your slimes ("blobs") paint tiles they move over.
- Fill as much of the floor with your colour as possible.
- Having a square of four tiles filled with your colour doubles the score of those tiles. They will visibly connect.
- Destroy your opponent's colour fields by running over them.

================================================================================
4. CONTROLS
Supported movement methods:

- WASD or Arrow Keys
- Mouse
- Gamepad

Toggle fullscreen by pressing F11 or Select on your Gamepad.
